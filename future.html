Future Aspirations

Short-term goals (1–2 years): In the short term, my goal is to strengthen my programming knowledge and gain practical experience through academic projects, internships, and continuous learning. I aim to improve my skills in software development, problem-solving, and modern programming technologies while completing my degree successfully. Mid-term goals (3–5 years): Within the next three to five years, I plan to establish myself as a professional software engineer working in a technology-driven organization where I can contribute to building reliable and innovative software solutions. During this period, I also intend to pursue additional certifications and specialized training to deepen my expertise. Long-term goals (Impact and purpose): In the long term, my goal is to use my technical knowledge to develop technological solutions that address real-world challenges, particularly in developing communities. I aspire to create impactful systems and applications that improve access to services, enhance efficiency, and contribute positively to society while mentoring others who are pursuing careers in technology.

Career goals

Short-term

My short-term goal is to secure an internship or entry-level opportunity where I can gain practical experience in software development and real-world project environments. Currently, I am building skills in programming, web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and strengthening my problem-solving and debugging abilities. I am also improving my teamwork and communication skills to prepare for collaborative professional settings.

Mid-term

My mid-term goal is to become a full-time software engineer in a reputable company where I can contribute to complex software projects and continue growing professionally. I plan to pursue certifications in advanced programming languages and frameworks, as well as participate in training programs that enhance my technical and leadership skills.

Long-term

My long-term goal is to become a senior software engineer or technical lead, where I can guide development teams and contribute to impactful projects. I also aim to be involved in community initiatives that promote technology education and support underrepresented groups in the tech industry.

Values that guide me

Growth: I improve through learning and feedback.
Integrity: I do the right thing even when it’s hard.
Impact: I aim to solve real problems for people.
